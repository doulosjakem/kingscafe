At the King's Cafe, we strive to make the best coffee you have ever had, exactly how you want it. With our dedication to serving our community and belief that Christ is glorified in our craft we aim to provide you with a cup of coffee made from fresh roasted high quality beans. Most coffee goes stale a month after it's roasted even with fancy packaging, by roasting our beans in small batchs in-house we are able to provide you with fresh roasted flavors such as:

Tanzanian Peaberry

Medium roast with a medium-light body accompanied by a rich chocolate flavor and intense brightness. Subtle notes of wine, citrus and tart fruit with a soft sweet finish.

Guatemalan Antigua

Lightly roasted and full-bodied with a smooth finish. From high altitude volcanic soil, this roast is rich in flavor and aroma. With notes of cocoa, berry, and citrus.

Colombian

Dark roast with a full-body roasted to perfection in-house. Rustic sweetness and a smooth profile. Notes of cocoa and dark fruit.

White Peaberry

Our own ultra light roast of peaberry beans from Tanzania produces a very unique coffee experience with about 3x the caffeine of a normal roast. This is a King's Cafe specialty for the adventurous.
